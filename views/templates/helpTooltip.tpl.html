Help - basic instructions on using this site

- click in order to open a .gpx/.kml/.geojson file.
- click in order to save your work to a file.
- click in order to move the map to your current location.
- click in order to toggle route drawing. use escape to stop, and double click to remove a point.
- click in order to toggle routing.
- click in order to toggle marker drawing.
- click in order to undo last action.
- click in order to clear all elements.
- click in order to edit the map in OSM.
- click in order to see more information.
- click in order to open this dialog.

Hope you'll enjoy it!
